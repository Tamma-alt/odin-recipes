Jollof Recipe

Description

This traditional Nigerian dish brings out the best possible flavor of rice and chicken. Chicken is first sauteed on the stove top to produce a wonderful aromatic base for the rice. For an extra special and authentic touch, serve jollof rice with fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red bell pepper / tatashe
  • 500ml chopped tomatoes
  • 100g tomato purée
  • 2 scotch bonnet
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • Maggi
  • Salt
  • 1 teaspoon curry
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger
  • 2 cups water or stock

Steps

  1. Blend your tomatoes, scotch bonnet, and tatashe together.
  2. Put your rice into a bowl and soak with hot water.
  3. Heat up your oil.
  4. Add your chopped onions and allow them to fry.
  5. Add and fry your blended ingredients and purée.
  6. Add your maggi, garlic, ginger, curry, thyme and salt.
  7. Pour your rice into the pot and mix together.
  8. Cook on low medium heat.
  9. Cook until soft.
  10. Serve the rice.
