Lasagna

Description

This enchilada lasagna is perfect for dinner or potlucks.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1.4 pounds ground turkey
  • 1 (28 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can died tomatoes with lime juice and clantro
  • 1 (16 ounce) packages small-curd cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 5 (6 inch) corn tortillas, halved
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • cooking spray
  • 1 green onion, diced
