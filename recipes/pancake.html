Pancake Recipe

Description

This pancake recipe is easy to make and only requires a few simple ingredients you probably have in your kitchen already

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • salt
  • Milk
  • Melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together
  2. Melt butter and warm milk
  3. Whisk wet ingredients together
  4. Combine the dry and wet ingredients
  5. Cook on a hot buttered skillet or pan
  6. Enjoy!
